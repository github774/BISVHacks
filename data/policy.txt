Mandatory resource rationing by behavior score. Because the state can “provide everything,” it assigns each person a single “access score” based on compliance (travel restrictions, vehicle rules, internet use, etc.). Above a score of X, you get full housing, healthcare, and education; below it, you get reduced or suspended access. Everyone must live under a single behavioral standard, so even obedient citizens can be downgraded overnight if definitions of “compliance” change. Even infinite resources are no help when access is controlled by a single score everyone depends on.
