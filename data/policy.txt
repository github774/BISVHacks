The government will implement comprehensive immigration enforcement policies, including increased workplace raids, border security measures, and expedited deportations for undocumented immigrants. This policy will eliminate sanctuary city protections and require local law enforcement to cooperate with federal immigration authorities. Additionally, the policy restricts access to public services such as healthcare, housing assistance, and education for undocumented individuals and their families. Legal immigration pathways will be narrowed, with stricter requirements for work visas and family reunification. The policy aims to reduce illegal immigration but will significantly impact immigrant communities, mixed-status families, and industries that rely on immigrant labor such as agriculture, construction, and service sectors.
